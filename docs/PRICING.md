## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing

**Q: How am I billed?**
A: Monthly recurring via credit card or annual invoice (10% discount).

**Q: Can I change tiers mid-month?**
A: Yes! Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades apply next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer annual contracts?**
A: Yes! **10% discount** for annual prepayment (Business and Enterprise tiers).

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit card (Stripe), ACH, wire transfer (Enterprise).

### Usage & Limits

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?**
A: Vectors: Pay overage fees. QPS: Throttled with 429 errors. Storage: Automatic archival to cold tier.

**Q: Can I increase limits temporarily?**
A: Yes! Contact support for burst capacity (Business and Enterprise).

**Q: Do you offer volume discounts?**
A: Yes! 20% discount for 100M+ vectors (Enterprise tier).

### Data & Security

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Multi-region by default (US-East-1, US-West-2, EU-West-1). Custom regions available (Enterprise).

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
A: Yes! AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, mTLS for service mesh.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! API-based export anytime. Bulk export available (Business and Enterprise).

**Q: Do you offer HIPAA compliance?**
A: Yes! Business tier is HIPAA-ready. Enterprise tier includes BAA.

### Support

**Q: What support channels are available?**
A: Free: Discord. Startup: Email. Business: Priority email + Slack. Enterprise: 24/7 dedicated Slack + on-call.

**Q: Do you offer migration assistance?**
A: Yes! Free migration support for Enterprise. $5,000 flat fee for lower tiers.

**Q: Can I get a dedicated account manager?**
A: Yes! Included with Business and Enterprise tiers.
